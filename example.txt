First boil the kettle
Second wash and dry the cups, tea tastes better in clean cups
Third scald the cup by pouring in boiling water
Fourth put the teabag in the cup and pour the boiling water directly on top of the teabag
Leave the tea to brew for about 3-5 minutes 
Once the tea is brewed, remove the teabag with a teaspoon, making sure to squeeze the teabag with firm pressure agaist the side of the cup
Add milk to tast. Good, strong tea should be the mahogany in colour.
Make sure the cup is cleap is clean - there shouldn't be any moisture on the bottom, or tea splashes on the outside or the rim. Use a teatowel to touch up if neccesary.
